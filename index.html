Sam Agres' Paintings

Sam Agres was a prolific painter based in Fort Wayne, Indiana, whose dedication to his craft resulted in a remarkable body of work exceeding 3,000 pieces. His paintings, often rooted in the landscapes and life of the Midwest, reflect a deep connection to his surroundings and a disciplined artistic practice. Over the decades, Sam’s work resonated with local communities and was featured in exhibitions such as those hosted by the Wunderkammer Company.

This website hosts a curated selection of approximately 800 of Sam’s paintings, serving as a tribute to his creative spirit. Through this digital gallery, we aim to preserve and share his legacy with art enthusiasts and the broader public.

This website is in progress, paintings will be cropped and rotated soon.
